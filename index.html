Fun Facts About Cheetahs

  • They are the fastest land animal on Earth.
  • Cheetahs are the oldest cat species that are still alive.
  • A Cheetahs tail acts like a rudder.

    • The Sad Side Of Cheetahs.

  • Sadly Cheetahs are an endangered animal, which means if we don't take care of them well,they will die out soon.
  • They have weak jaws and weak canine teeth that means they can't bite into their prey easily,which results them not having food.
  • They are slowly losing their habitat because of us humans.
  • Lions, lepards, hyena's are all big hunters for baby cheetahs which ruins the cheetahs life in the future.
